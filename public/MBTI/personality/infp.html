INFP - The Mediator

The Mediator

Overview

This is a sample overview for the INFP personality type. INFPs are known for their unique traits. They often excel in various areas and may struggle with certain challenges.

Strengths

  • Analytical and strategic thinking
  • Strong problem-solving skills
  • Independent and self-motivated
  • Creative and innovative

Challenges

  • Can be overly critical
  • May struggle with emotional expression
  • Can be perfectionistic
